Privacy Policy

  • webOS Archive uses cookies on its web pages for a local analytics service to understand traffic and site activity.
  • Some of webOS Archive apps use a cookie to remember login information, if the users opts to do so.
  • Some of webOS Archive services store user data, if the user chooses to provide that data.
  • All webOS Archive services can be self-hosted, if the user wishes to maintain control over their data.
  • webOS Archive will comply with legal requests for user data if compelled to do so by law.
  • Otherwise, webOS Archive will not sell or give away data in any form, to anyone ever for any reason.
